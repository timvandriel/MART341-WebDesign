How to make pumpkin seeds

By Tim Van Driel

Materials | Step 1 | Step 2 | Step 3 | Step 4

What you'll need:

  • Pumpkin seeds
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Cookie sheet
  • Oven

Step 1: Clean the seeds

Place the seeds in a colander and rinse them under cold water. Remove any pumpkin guts that may be stuck to the seeds.

Step 2: Dry the seeds

Place the seeds on a paper towel and pat them dry. Make sure they are completely dry before moving on to the next step.

Step 3: Season the seeds

Place the seeds in a bowl and add 1 tablespoon of olive oil and 1 teaspoon of salt. Mix the seeds until they are evenly coated.

Step 4: Bake the seeds

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Spread the seeds evenly on a cookie sheet and bake for 10 minutes. Remove the seeds from the oven and stir them. Place the seeds back in the oven and bake for another 10 minutes. Remove the seeds from the oven and let them cool. Enjoy!
