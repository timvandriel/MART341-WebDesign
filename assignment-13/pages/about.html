About Us

At Landscapes R Us we are a group of passionate individuals who want to improve your space. With a passion for creating outdoor spaces that inspire and rejuvenate, we have been serving Missoula, Montana and its surrounding areas with top-notch landscaping services since 1889.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to transform ordinary outdoor spaces into extraordinary landscapes that reflect the beauty of nature and enhance the quality of life for our clients. Whether it's a residential garden, a commercial property, or a community park, we approach every project with creativity, expertise, and a commitment to excellence.
